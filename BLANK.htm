The making of the ACTION FIGURE (summer 2014)

  • 112 PARTS + 41 SPARE PARTS of red PLA

    The chosen material was PLA, even if the initial model is made of ABS, we like challenges!

  • Assembling the central pieces with spine

    The spine is made of a rope of melted PLA, and placed through the central parts.

  • Filing and gluing work

    The heaviest part of the work, with a lot of material, testing our endurance! The baby's taking shape.

  • Setting up the 4 members

    The articulations of the members are assembled with maleable metal spikes, or just sloted in.

  • 112 parts - 41 spare parts - 70 articulations - 47 cm / 18,5 in

    Model of the open source action figure by Jason Welch (ABS) / Thinkiverse.com
