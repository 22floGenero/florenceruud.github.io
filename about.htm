MARS 2014

-


GENERO was completely re-designed and reduced.

 . The Newsletter & general news section are suppressed, the principal updates are gathered in
the UPDATES section.


 . The website displays an Artwork section with: 2D and 3D works, releases.


 . You can also find the following paths: Links, Misc, playlist, Sub_sites.


 . This site has incredibly evolved through the years. From a large board filled with china ink work,
airbrush material and sheets, to the vast music/artwork and poetry portal it was in the 2000's,
and the present Genero Portal, the common link is still ... design.


 . 2d, 3d, ink, airbrush, sketch, drawings, mesh or webdesign, it's still the need to build, organize, create,
present, & represent in an aesthetic way. Yes, Genero portal is growing again, step by step during
the few free moments we find here and there.


. Naturally it's not complete, it's not responsive (yet) & it can be improved, and done without CMS, indeed.
Drop me a mail if you find a bug, I'll fix it asap.


 . Design, webdesign & 3D printing, it's an activity we develop beside work, life & all in between.
 . Yes, I have a port-folio and an amount of Artworks that is quite solid, but I don't that they are relevant
here on Genero. Polyvalence & creativity are the words.
