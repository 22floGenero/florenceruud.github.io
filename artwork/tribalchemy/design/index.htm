When it comes to Design...
The results are quite prolific some years,
and rather sporadic other years.
The design are often made to satisfy an order, a wish, an idea that seemed quite great then.
But time passed on and there are plenty of releases that don't fit my views, my beliefs,
or simply my taste anymore.
It's also thrilling to begin again and again with a blank ark,
building, starting up, developing are notions that are here often stronger than collecting ... dust.
