Words & thoughts ...
For those who'll be able to see, even to apreciate my Artworks, for the achieved experience that each one represents; The further step towards a progression, a search of improvement, of both the techniques, the picture & the message present in the Artwork.
The artist creates a pictural world in opening his own frontiers, feelings, to reach the exact stadium which reveals the vision of its creator.
Each designer uses his sensibility for expressing a visual ideal, a glimpse of a thought, an idea, a dream. Whatever the technique used. The quality depends of the sincerity of the artist, how far he's able to share his interior universe...
