- WORDS -

In the world of lists, playlists, and sortings there aren't any boundaries. The temptation is strong to list the best wines, games, films, books, and then stamps, shoes and why not stones you encounter on your way. But there is more fetich than an act of creation in listing and ranging meticulously items
like an insect's collection.
This is the will to shape a bit order in the chaos that shapes the world.
The desire to build a progression like an artist showing his photographs chronologically. You'll find as well colorfull exhibitions in the other corners of this site. The goal is to create, with an aesthetic touch, and with the tools available at each period. The evolution is nowadays exponential, and 3D printing will soon become a weapon a mass consumption. The innovative art will be replaced by daily utility, with a easier and practical printer in each home. You'll replace your fork, your pen's cap, your shoes
and even your children's puzzle in an instant.
The art of adapting our everfaster changing environment will become an important skill.
The art itself is also transforming, from pictural supports to digital ones, from static to animated scenes, even from linear to spatial lines. 3D printing already offers 3D drawind pens, that allow the artists to create beautiful and fragile shapes in the air. Most of the screen applications also take the same direction, and the old holograms are back in the game.
3D is also the emerging demand for 3D reality games, as Occulus Rift devices and accessories, simulators and even panoramic cinema 4D or virtual chirurgical operations. The applications are endless and it is up to us to support, develop, even anticipate them in a way that can remain constructive, entertaining and inspiring for us as human beings.
